# Employee-Recruitment-Training-Performance-Analysis-Dashboard-Power-BI-

This project is a comprehensive HR Analytics Dashboard built using Power BI, designed to analyze data across the entire employee lifecycle—from recruitment and onboarding to training participation and employee performance. The dashboard provides HR teams, business leaders, and decision-makers with actionable workforce insights through interactive visualizations and structured metrics.

# Project Overview
Organizations rely on HR data to track workforce productivity, optimize recruitment strategies, evaluate training outcomes, and identify improvement areas. This Power BI Dashboard consolidates multiple HR datasets and transforms them into a clear, visual, and interactive analytical solution.

# The project is divided into three major dashboards:

# Summary Report (Workforce Overview)

This section provides a high-level overview of the organization's employees, highlighting distribution across job types, employee status, and performance outcomes.
Key Insights
Total Employees: 1533
Employee Types: Full-Time, Part-Time, Contract
Top-Rated Employees: 137
Employees Needing Improvement: 398
Termination Type Insights: Involuntary, Voluntary, Retirement, Resignation
Pay Zone Categorization: Zone A, Zone B, Zone C
Department-Level Distribution: Production, IT/IS, Sales, Software Engineering, Admin Offices, Executive Office
Job Status Overview: Active, Terminated, Future Start, Leave of Absence
This dashboard helps HR teams understand the overall health of the workforce, identify departments with high employee volumes, detect attrition patterns, and review performance categories.

# Interview & Recruitment Dashboard

This section focuses on the candidate management and hiring funnel.
Recruitment Metrics Included
Interview status tracking: Applied, In Review, Interviewing, Offered, Rejected
Education Level Distribution: PhD, Master's, Bachelor’s, High School
New Joiners: 718
Experts (Experienced Hires): 718
Job Title Filtering: Allows dynamic filtering by job roles

This dashboard helps recruitment teams track application flow, analyze qualification levels, identify slow stages in the hiring pipeline, and understand candidate conversion trends.

# Objective of the Project

The main goal is to provide HR decision-makers with:
     A data-driven understanding of recruitment efficiency
     Clear insights into employee performance and workforce distribution
     Improving training effectiveness and talent development strategies
     Supporting organizations in planning hiring, workforce allocation, and employee retention initiatives
